MA Public Records Request Assistant

How this works: Enter your name, your email address and your public records request. The form will compose an email within your email client, addressed to the public records request offices of each town in Western Massachusetts. Each town office will be blind carbon copied and will thus receive a separate email. You will be able to further edit the text, remove towns, and give a final review to the email within your email client before you send.
